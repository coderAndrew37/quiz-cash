Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quiz Center?

Quiz Center is a platform where you can test your knowledge and earn coins by completing quizzes.

How do I start a quiz?

Navigate to the quiz page, select a quiz, and click the "Start Quiz" button.

How can I withdraw my earnings?

You can go to the "Withdrawals" section from your user area and request a withdrawal of your earned coins.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you can reset it from the login page using the "Forgot Password" link.
